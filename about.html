About Us

our aspirations

MindBox aims to build functional literacy in students and empowers them with the 21st Century skills. We use interdisciplinary and experiential learning approach to teach Design and Creative Thinking, Visual Communication, Technology and Digital Skills.

Our objective is to prepare every student from Consumer to Creator, Problem Mindset to Solution Mindset and a Doing Mind to Thinking Mind and in the process, obtain their overall physical, social, emotional, moral and creative development.
