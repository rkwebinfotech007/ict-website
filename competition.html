Vax india championship

VEX India Championship is the national league of the International VEX Worlds Championship which will take place in the U.S. In this competition,several teams of young students, and their mentors, from different cities of India will compete against each other to earn the Title of VEX India Champion. Teams will play the game of Annual Challenge set by VEX Team using VEX IQ Robotics Kits. The competition encourages teamwork,leadership and problem solving among groups of students.

The VEX India Championship is one of the initiatives of MindBox aimed at facilitating Creative Design Thinking with the help of 21st Century Technology as a Tool.
